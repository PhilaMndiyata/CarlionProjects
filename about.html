About Us

Welcome to Boom Carlion Projects, your trusted electrical service provider serving the Mkhambathini and Pietermaritzburg areas since 2021. Founded by Sbonelo Mzolo, our company is committed to delivering high-quality electrical solutions tailored to meet the needs of both residential and commercial clients.


At Boom Carlion Projects, we specialize in a wide range of services, including fault finding, wiring, and distribution board (DB) installation. Our team of skilled electricians is dedicated to ensuring your safety and satisfaction through efficient and reliable workmanship. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail and commitment to using the latest technology and techniques in the electrical industry.


As a locally owned and operated business, we understand the unique challenges faced by our community. We strive to build long-lasting relationships with our clients, ensuring that every project we undertake is completed on time and within budget. Our reputation for quality service and customer care has made us a preferred choice for many households and businesses in the region.

Join us on our journey to illuminate your spaces with safety and style.


Contact us today for all your electrical needs!
